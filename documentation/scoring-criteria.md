# Scoring Criteria & Rubric

## Candidate: __________________
## Total Score: _____/100

### Section 1: Strategic Budget (20 pts)
- **Exceeds (18-20):** Identifies cost-saving opportunities without impacting velocity (e.g., reserved instances, sunsetting duplicates).
- **Meets (12-17):** Proposes simple cuts (e.g., reducing headcount or software licenses).
- **Below (<12):** Proposes cuts that would cripple operations.

### Section 2: Incident Response (25 pts)
- **Exceeds (22-25):** Prioritizes containment, communication, and legal compliance (GDPR/CCPA) immediately.
- **Meets (15-21):** Follows standard isolation protocols but misses communication.
- **Below (<15):** Proposes restoring from backup before isolating the breach.

### Section 3: Modernization Risks (25 pts)
- **Exceeds (22-25):** Identifies "Data Consistency" and "Team Skill Gap" with specific mitigation plans.
- **Meets (15-21):** Identifies general risks (e.g., "cost") but lacks specific mitigation.
- **Below (<15):** Does not identify architectural risks.

### Section 4: Vendor Choice (10 pts)
- **Exceeds (9-10):** Justifies choice based on existing stack, specific feature needs (e.g., OpenAI integration), or cost optimization.
- **Meets (6-8):** Gives a generic answer.
- **Below (<6):** No justification provided.

### Section 5: Executive Vision (20 pts)
- **Exceeds (18-20):** Articulates a clear ROI, aligns with revenue growth, and shows leadership vision.
- **Meets (12-17):** Describes technology upgrades but fails to link to business value.
- **Below (<12):** Too technical or too vague for an executive board.
