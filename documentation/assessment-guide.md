# CIO Pre-Assessment Guide

**Total Time:** 45 Minutes
**Role:** Chief Information Officer (CIO)

## Scenario
You have recently been hired as the CIO of "Endava Solutions," a mid-sized enterprise (500 employees) experiencing rapid growth. The company currently relies on legacy on-premise systems but has a mandate to become "cloud-first" within 18 months.

## Your Tasks
You have 45 minutes to review the provided documents and address the following critical areas. Please create your responses in the `/results` folder.

### Task 1: Strategic IT Budget (10 mins)
Review the `strategy/it-budget.md` file.
- **Action:** The CFO has asked you to cut the IT budget by 15% next year without sacrificing security or growth. Briefly outline a 3-point strategy (in `/results/budget-cut-strategy.md`) to achieve this.

### Task 2: Incident Response (10 mins)
Review the `security/incident-response.md` file.
- **Action:** A ransomware attack has just been detected. List the immediate "Critical Steps" (first 60 minutes) you would order your team to execute.

### Task 3: Architecture Modernization (10 mins)
Review the `architecture/modernization-roadmap.md` file.
- **Action:** Identify the top two risks of migrating the monolithic legacy ERP to microservices and how you would mitigate them. Post your answer in `/results/migration-risks.md`.

### Task 4: Vendor Assessment (5 mins)
Review the `strategy/vendor-assessment.md` file.
- **Action:** You are choosing between AWS and Azure. Which one do you lean towards and why? (Keep it brief).

### Task 5: Executive Summary (10 mins)
- **Action:** Draft a 1-paragraph executive summary (in `/results/executive-summary.md`) outlining your vision for NexGen Solutions' IT over the next 3 years. This should align technology with business growth.

## Scoring Criteria
Your responses will be evaluated on:
1.  **Strategic Alignment:** Does your solution support business goals?
2.  **Risk Management:** Did you identify and mitigate risks?
3.  **Technical Fluency:** Do you understand modern architecture (Cloud, APIs, Security)?
4.  **Communication:** Can you distill complex topics for executives?

**Upload your responses by pushing your changes to the `results` branch (or commit to main) before the 45-minute timer ends.**
