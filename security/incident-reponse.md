# Incident Response Plan (IRP)

## Activation Criteria
- Malware/Ransomware detected.
- Unauthorized access to sensitive PII.
- DDoS attack affecting revenue-generating services.

## Response Team
- **CISO**: Leads the investigation.
- **Legal**: Advises on breach notification laws.
- **PR**: Prepares external messaging.
- **CIO (You)**: Communicates to the Board and approves operational shutdowns.

## Immediate Actions (First 30 mins)
1. **Isolate:** Segment infected machines.
2. **Identify:** Determine the entry point.
3. **Communicate:** Notify the CEO and Head of Legal.
