Executive Summary — IT Vision, FY2025–2027

Over the next three years, Endava Solutions' IT organization will shift from a cost center managing legacy infrastructure to a platform that directly enables business growth. We will complete a phased, risk-managed migration off the monolithic on-premise ERP to a cloud-native, microservices architecture — sequenced deliberately so the highest-risk work (the financial ledger) happens only after we've proven the pattern on lower-risk domains and built the operational maturity to run it safely, with zero disruption to month-end close. This modernization is funded not despite the Board's 15% budget reduction but through it: cloud rightsizing and licensing renegotiation convert the cost-cutting mandate into the primary funding mechanism for the transformation itself, rather than competing with it. Security remains non-negotiable throughout — our incident response capability will mature alongside the architecture, not be deferred to "after migration." By the end of year three, Endava Solutions will operate on a scalable, API-first platform that can absorb continued headcount and revenue growth without a proportional increase in infrastructure spend, positioning IT as a growth enabler rather than a constraint.
